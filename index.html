ABOUT

TAPIRS

Fat Crypto Tapirs is the first group of 3333 bizarre tapirs all completely
handmade with no code. Owning a Fat Crypto Tapir unlocks unique
abilities such as laziness, inertia, and insatiability. Join the Tapirs and
you will learn the most important techniques of physical and spiritual
abandonment. Who knows, maybe you will be the lord of the slackers!




STORY

The Fat Crypto Tapirs were born in November 2021, at the Polino house
while the 2 creators of the collection were looking for a way to make money.
NFTs were depopulating on the internet, so they decided to create the
first and only collection of tapirs that can be found on any NFT platform.
BRING THE TAPIRS TO THE MOON!




CRYPTO

The cryptocurrency world is very complex and fascinating, the Fat Crypto
Tapirs collection develops from the Polygon blockchain that connects to
that of Ethereum. In this way, those who want to buy an NFT will not have
to pay any taxes beyond the normal price. As the collection grows it also
increases in value and therefore prices gradually increase until they reach
a maximum ceiling of 50$ when the first 1000 tapirs are reached.
